How to use
  1. Click on runs button. They are numbered as 0, 1, 2, 4, 5 and 6.
  2. If its a extra, click on wide or NoBall or Byes. You have to click on run to enable this.Click 0 for no runs.
  3. If the batsman is out, click on out or run out or retired. You have to click on run to enable this. Click 0 for no runs.
  4. If the batsman is run out or retired, it will ask for which batsman and select the correct batsman who is out
  5. Click submit score to update the score. Please make sure you make no mistakes.
  6. If you have to change strike, click on strike button.
  7. If you want to change batsman, click on batsman button.
  8. If you want to change bowler, click on bowler button.
  9. You can see full scorecard by clicking on scorecard link.
  10. The innings will be over after all balls are bowled or all wickets fell. You can also end innings by clicking the button at top.
  11. The match will be over after all balls are bowled or all wickets fell or second team wins. You can also end match by clicking the button at top.
  12. Batsman and Bowler will change as per runs scored or over bowled or wicket fell.
